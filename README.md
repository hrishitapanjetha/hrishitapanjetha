## Hi there 👋

Currently pursuing a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence and honestly having the time of my life turning caffeine and compute power into things that (hopefully) make sense. I’m obsessed with using tech to crack open real-world problems, the kind that keep people up at night, not just the kind that give GPUs nightmares.

My playground is machine learning, deep learning, nlp, ai agents, llms, and computer vision. Nothing beats that feeling when a model starts actually behaving and helps push technology a little closer to the future. I love that sweet spot between theory and application — where math meets creativity and you suddenly find yourself explaining to non-tech folks that “no, the model is not sentient… yet.”

I come from a pretty solid base in stats, web dev, and neural networks that are much deeper than my sleep schedule most days. What really drives me is the idea that AI isn’t just cool, it can actually improve lives. Whether it’s smarter healthcare tools, more natural human-computer interaction, or scalable solutions for those massive global problems we all pretend aren’t stressing us out… I’m in.

I’m actively looking to team up with developers, researchers and innovators who are building things that feel a little bit like magic (but with math). My long-term mission? To be one of those AI people who not only build impressive models but also make systems that genuinely help people, without adding any scary “sky turning red” energy to the world.

Always open to connecting with anyone who’s passionate about pushing AI forward, responsibly, creatively, and with just the right amount of nerdy enthusiasm.
